SE2 Team Sync

SE2 Team Sync is an internal tool used by Salesforce Solution Engineers to synchronize display names, avatar photos, and department metadata between a Salesforce demo org and a Slack Enterprise Grid workspace.

What it does

The tool reads a central CSV of Japanese team members, then writes matching display names, avatars, and department fields to both Salesforce demo orgs (via sf data update record and Connect API) and Slack Enterprise Grid (via SCIM v1/v2 PATCH endpoints).

Scope

Internal Salesforce SE use only. Not distributed to end users or offered as a commercial service.
